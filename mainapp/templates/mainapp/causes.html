Our   work

Working towards empowering communities to be able to address the most significant things affecting their
wellbeing through. Community health programming, education and training and Research and development.

We are Passionate about helping communities develop the skills they need to make positive life changes.

Reducing new HIV infection and Gender Based Violence among Adolescents Girls and Young Women by 40%

Most people living with or at risk of contracting HIV do not have access to prevention, care, or treatment.

Supporting children and Families affected or infected with HIV & AIDS

Regular HIV testing, treatment, monitoring and care for children living with HIV can enable them to live long and fulfilling lives.

Empowering Girls in Decision making - She Leads.

The She Leads consortium aims to increase sustained influence of girls and young women (GYW) on decision-making and the transformation of gender norms in formal and informal institutions.



Enhancing Access to Education - Child Sponsorship Project.

Pamoja supports school going children by providing bursaries, uniforms, supplies and mentorship.

Sustainable Livelihoods.

Empowering community members to move out of the poverty cycle.

Safe Water Projects.

Pamoja CBO collaborated with local communities to plan, implement, and evaluate safe water projects.





Partnering for incremental change & development - Research & Innovation Projects.

Our organization has also collaborated with various parties in conducting research with the aim to improve innovation and increase change and development.

Bringing Back "The Youth Sanity" into the Community - Pamoja Youth Center.

We have designed a whole-of-society response to mental health strains, which discretely spiraled in the wake of COVID-19 resulting in a constantly deteriorating mental health impact.

Caregivers Sensitization.

Families Matter! I and II Program is an Evidence Informed Behavioral Intervention (EBI) which was adapted from Families Matter!
