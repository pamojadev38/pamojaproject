Our
Impact

Your generosity makes it possible for our work to create meaningful change that touches countless lives. Whether it's a one-time gift or a recurring donation, every dollar goes directly to our mission of promoting Healthier, Happier and Able Communities.


15,687
Beneficiaries

Pamoja Community Based Organization, through its programs serves a total of 15,687 direct beneficiaries including PEPFAR supported 3,722 adolescent girls and young women, 5,886 orphans and vulnerable children and additional 3,104 enrolled in child sponsorship program supported by Plan International.

19,621
HIV Tests

A total of 11,091 (84%) of our direct beneficiaries received HIV test including children, adolescents and young women aged 20-24

2,718
School Fees

We provided school fees support to over 2,000 children in secondary schools

5,311   Sanitary Pads

We have provided year round sanitary pads and sex education to over 1,325 which has helped girls gain more school days and cub absenteeism

2,022   Contraceptives

More than 1,049 girls and young women have been initiated in contraceptives

531   Social Protection

We gave out unconditional cash transfer to a total of 472 young women aged 20-24

3,723   School Uniforms

More than 3,723 children in secondary and primary schools have received school uniforms and other scholastic materials

2,400
Early Child Development

More than 600 children benefited from our early child development and education (ECD) support

2,604
Birth Registrations

Over 1,204 children were facilitated to acquire birth certificates

530   Prep

We have initiated 391 young women (15-24) on HIV Pre exposure prophylaxis to reduce their risk to contracting HIV.
